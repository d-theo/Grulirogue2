Welcome adventurer, your cat has wandered off and fallen into a space vortex!!! You must summon all your courage for he needs you! Travel through time, defeat obstacles and bring kitty home safely. But beware for a false move and kitty will be lost for good.
How to play:
* [Arrows] to move
* [w] to pass your turn
* [f] to attack, [z] to switch target when attacking (or [Arrows]), [Esc] to cancel
* [i] to display inventory, [Esc] or [i] to leave it
* [g] to display skills, [Enter] to select and cast one
* [>] to go to the next level when sitting on stairs
